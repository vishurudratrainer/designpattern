     the snapshots.             (Cannot be read).           (Cannot look inside).
The Originator (TextEditor): This is the active object that holds the current text.
It is the only character that knows how to create a snapshot or read a snapshot.

The Memento (EditorMemento): This is the actual snapshot.
Think of it as a locked vault. Inside this vault is the text history,
 but the vault has no keys or public buttons on the outside.

The Caretaker (HistoryManager): This is the history timeline tracker.
It maintains a list or a Stack of vaults.
 Crucially, the Caretaker cannot open the vault or see what text is inside.
 It just holds onto the boxes and hands them back when requested.
